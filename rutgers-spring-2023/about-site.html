Form and Meaning
Rutgers Spring 2023
about this site

This website is the syllabus for a design course at Rutgers University, Mason Gross School of the Arts in Spring 2023. It serves as a web portal for learning resources and class documentation.

The web design and syllabus is adapted from Mindy Seu's Design for the Net. Lecture slides, project/exercise briefs, and teaching material is credited to Mindy Seu, Jacqueline Thaw, Sasha Portis, Sophie Auger, and other instructors.
